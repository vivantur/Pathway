# Specific Familiars — rules source text

Supplied by the project owner (2026-07-11), per the rules-from-source policy.
Primary source: Player Core 2 pg. 170 plus the listed books. Mechanical values
preserved exactly; long flavor prose condensed.

## Rules (Player Core 2 pg. 170)

Some familiars are more powerful than the usual familiars, and possess unique
abilities. Any character can gain a specific familiar so long as they already
have a familiar with at least the required number of abilities listed in the
specific familiar's stat block. This transition requires no downtime and has
no cost. Once you've selected a specific familiar, you can't change it without
losing your familiar.

The granted abilities entry lists normal familiar and master abilities that
familiar has. The familiar also gains unique abilities listed below the
granted abilities entry. You can never swap out any of these granted or unique
abilities. If your familiar gains more abilities than the required number, you
can use the remaining abilities to select additional familiar and master
abilities as normal. Statistics and abilities not listed use the normal
familiar rules.

## Catalog

Format: name — source (traits; rarity/access). Required N. Granted: …
Unique abilities follow.

### Aeon Wyrd — Player Core 2 pg. 170 (construct)
Required 3. Granted: construct, flier.
- Aeon Stone Reservoir: can house any aeon stone; you gain its benefits without investing it, plus its resonant power. Doesn't interfere with an invested wayfinder.
- Can't Walk: no land Speed.
- Crystalline: weakness to sonic equal to your level.

### Calligraphy Wyrm — Rival Academies pg. 44 (dragon; uncommon; Cobyslarni/Pathfinder Society)
Required 6. Granted: darkvision, flier, manual dexterity, scent, skilled (arcana, society), speech.
- Ink Spray [1a, arcane, once per 10 min]: 10-foot cone; Reflex save vs master's spell/class DC (higher). Crit success unaffected; success: if invisible, concealed instead for 2 rounds; failure: as success 1 minute; crit failure: as success 10 minutes + blinded 1 round (or until wiped, Interact).
- Stylus Claws: stylus-shaped claws filled with natural ink; can write without buying ink.

### Ceru — Impossible Lands pg. 220 (beast; legacy)
Required 4. Granted: cantrip connection (guidance, mage hand), darkvision, touch telepathy.
- Created Magic: grants mage hand + guidance via cantrip connection; replace off-list cantrips with ones from your list.
- Turn of Fate [2a, divination, once per day, range 30 ft]: shift one target's next attack roll/save/skill check. Bad fortune: Will save vs your class/spell DC (misfortune) — failure: roll twice take worse; crit failure: affected for next three attempts. Good fortune: roll twice take better (fortune). Immune 1 day after.

### Clockwork Familiar — Grand Bazaar pg. 23 (clockwork, construct; uncommon; legacy)
Required 3. Granted: darkvision.
- Clockwork: must be wound (1 minute) for 24 hours of operation; enemies can Disable a Device (standard DC) to drain 1 hour (2 on crit). Rewinding anytime restores to 24h.
- Constructed: immune to bleed, death effects, disease, doomed, drained, fatigued, healing, necromancy, nonlethal attacks, paralyzed, poison, sickened, unconscious; HP recovered only via Repair; destroyed at 0 HP.
- Electricity Vulnerability: weakness to electricity equal to your level.
- Steam Screen [1a, once per minute; costs 1 hour of operational time]: +1 circumstance to its Intimidation; its square gains concealment (no Hide/Sneak from it) for 1 round.
- Toggle Standby Mode [2a]: standby (no time drain, senses at –2 Perception, can't act until Commanded out).

### Crawling Hand — Book of the Dead pg. 40 (undead; legacy)
Required 5. Granted: manual dexterity, spell delivery, valet.
- Lend a Hand: gains 1 reaction per turn usable only to Aid an attack roll by an ally sharing its space; the Aid automatically succeeds.
- Undead: negative healing; immune to death effects, disease, poison, unconscious; destroyed at 0 HP.

### Cullitox Shardling — Rage of Elements pg. 42 (earth elemental)
Required 3. Granted: burrower, elemental (earth only), speech.
- Crystal Scent: senses crystals/gems within 60 feet as scent.

### Dweomercat Cub — PFS Guide pg. 122 (beast; legacy)
Required 4. Granted: darkvision, scent, speech.
- Alter Dweomer [1a, abjuration/arcane, once per 10 min; requires being targeted by/within a spell since its last turn]: gains an effect by the school (abjuration +1 AC; conjuration fog cloud 5-ft burst; divination +1 skills; enchantment +1 saves; evocation 1d6 force per 2 levels to the caster, basic Reflex vs your class/spell DC; illusion invisible; necromancy temp HP = your level; transmutation +1 attacks) for 1d4 rounds.
- Detect Magic: casts 1st-level detect magic as an arcane innate spell.

### Elemental Scamp — Rage of Elements pg. 43 (elemental)

- Elemental Mobility: air flier / earth burrower / fire jet / metal levitator / water amphibious / wood climber.
- Scamp Elements (fixed at selection): air slashing; earth bludgeoning; fire fire; metal slashing; water acid; wood (plant+wood) poison.

### Homunculus — Player Core 2 pg. 171 (construct)

### House Drake — Shadows at Sundown pg. 53 (dragon; uncommon; legacy)
Required 6. Granted: darkvision, flier, manual dexterity, speech.

- Silver Infusion [2a, arcane/transmutation]: one of your weapons counts as silver until your next turn. Shares the 1d4-round lockout.
- Tenacious Mind: once per day if the drake took no actions last round — Ferocious Will [reaction]: on your successful save vs a magical mental effect, deal 2d6 mental to the source (basic Will vs your class/spell DC; failure also slowed 1).

### Imp — Player Core 2 pg. 171 (fiend, unholy)
Required 7. Granted: darkvision, flier, manual dexterity, resistance (poison), skilled (deception), speech, touch telepathy.

- Imp Invisibility: once per hour, casts invisibility on itself (divine innate).
