# Companion type stat blocks — rules source text

Stat blocks supplied by the project owner (2026-07-11) for building out
`packages/core`'s companion catalog, per the rules-from-source policy.
Transcribed from the owner's paste: all mechanical values are preserved
exactly; some Support Benefit / Advanced Maneuver prose is lightly condensed
(marked entries follow the original wording closely — when in doubt, ask the
owner for the original text before relying on nuance). Format per entry:
name — source (rarity/access); Size; HP (ancestry); attribute modifiers;
attacks; skill; senses; speed; special; Support Benefit; Advanced Maneuver.
`[1a]/[2a]/[R]` = one action / two actions / reaction.

### Air Elemental — Rage of Elements pg. 40 (uncommon; storm order)
Size Small; HP 4; Str +1, Dex +2, Con +1, Int -4, Wis +1, Cha +1
Attacks: gust (finesse) 1d6 bludgeoning
Skill Stealth; Senses darkvision; Speed fly 50 feet
Support: Your air elemental swirls around you with concealing gusts, making it harder for your foes to hit you and possibly giving you a moment to hide. Until the start of your next turn, if you hit with a Strike and deal damage to a creature in your air elemental's reach, you are concealed to that creature until the start of your next turn.
Advanced Maneuver — Circling Flyby [2a]: The air elemental Flies up to half its Speed, makes two gust Strikes, then Flies up to half its Speed again to return to its original location. Both attacks count toward the air elemental's multiple attack penalty, but the penalty doesn't increase until after it makes both attacks.
