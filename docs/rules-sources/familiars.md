# Familiars — rules source text

Verbatim rules text supplied by the project owner (2026-07-11), per the repo's
rules-from-source policy. Source: Player Core pg. 212–214 plus the listed

## Familiars (Player Core pg. 212)

Familiars are mystically bonded creatures tied to your magic. Most familiars
were originally animals, though the ritual of becoming a familiar makes them
something more. You gain the Pet general feat, except that your pet has
special abilities. Common choices for familiars include bats, cats, foxes,
ravens, and snakes. Some familiars are different, usually described in the
ability that granted you a familiar; for example, a druid's leshy familiar has
the plant or fungus trait instead of animal. A familiar is different from a
basic pet in the following ways.

### Modifiers and AC

For Perception, Acrobatics, and Stealth, you can have your familiar use your
spellcasting attribute modifier + your level instead of 3 + your level if it's
higher.

### Communication

Your familiar can communicate empathically with you as long as it's within 1
mile of you, sharing emotions. It doesn't understand or speak languages
normally, but it can gain speech from a familiar ability.

### Selecting Familiar and Master Abilities

Instead of choosing two abilities only when you gain your familiar as you do
with a normal pet, you can choose the two abilities each day during your daily
preparations. You can choose from familiar abilities and master abilities. You
can choose the pet abilities from the feat as familiar abilities as well. You
can't swap out abilities that are innate to your familiar. For example, you
couldn't choose not to give a raven familiar flying.

A familiar can have no more than one ability that changes its creature trait
(such as construct or plant).

## Familiar Abilities

- **Accompanist** (Player Core pg. 212): Whenever you attempt a Performance
  check, if your familiar is nearby and can act, it accompanies you. This
  grants you a +1 circumstance bonus, or +2 if you're a master in Performance.
- **Alchemical Gut** (Treasure Vault Remastered pg. 51): Your familiar can act
  as a bomb coagulant alembic by swallowing the bomb to be affected (two
  Interact actions from it and one from you); it regurgitates the concentrated
  item 1 minute later. If the bomb's level is higher than your level –1, it
  instead takes damage as though struck by the bomb.
- **Ambassador** (Grand Bazaar pg. 34): Despite being a minion, your familiar
  gains 1 reaction at the start of its turns, usable only to Aid you on a
  Diplomacy check to Make an Impression. It automatically succeeds at its
  check to Aid (critically succeeds if you're a master).
- **Amphibious** (Player Core pg. 259): Gains the amphibious trait; land and
  swim Speeds each equal to its highest land/swim Speed.
- **Burrower** (Player Core pg. 259): Gains a burrow Speed of 5 feet.
- **Climber** (Player Core pg. 259): Gains a climb Speed of 25 feet.
- **Construct** (Player Core pg. 212): Has the construct trait instead of
  animal. Immune to death effects, disease, doomed, drained, fatigued,
  healing, nonlethal attacks, paralyzed, poison, sickened, spirit,
  unconscious, vitality, and void. Requires the tough ability.
- **Damage Avoidance** (Player Core pg. 212): Choose one type of save. The
  familiar takes no damage when it rolls a success on that save type.
- **Darkeater** (Secrets of Magic pg. 229): After 10 consecutive minutes in
  dim light or darkness, recovers HP equal to half your level. (Shadow
  familiars; shadowcasters may pick it for any familiar.)
- **Darkvision** (Player Core pg. 259): Gains darkvision.
- **Dragon** (Player Core pg. 212): Has the dragon trait instead of animal.
- **Echolocation** (Player Core pg. 259): Hearing as a precise sense within
  20 feet.
- **Elemental** (Player Core pg. 212): Elemental trait instead of animal;
  choose air/earth/fire/metal/water/wood as an added trait. Immune to bleed,
  paralyzed, poison, sleep, and its element. Requires the resistance ability.
- **Fast Movement** (Player Core pg. 259): Increase one Speed from 25 to 40 ft.
- **Flier** (Player Core pg. 259): Gains a fly Speed of 25 feet.
- **Focused Rejuvenation** (Player Core pg. 212): Regains 1 HP per your level
  whenever you Refocus.
- **Fungus** (Player Core pg. 212): Fungus trait instead of animal.
- **Gills** (Grand Bazaar pg. 34): Can breathe water in addition to air.
- **Greater Resistance** (Grand Bazaar pg. 34): Resistance from the resistance
  ability becomes 3 + half your level. Requires resistance.
- **Independent** (Player Core pg. 212): In an encounter, if you don't Command
  it, it still gains 1 action each round.
- **Item Delivery** (Player Core 2 pg. 170): Command it (adjacent) to take a
  light-Bulk item you hold, take one move action, and pass or administer it to
  a willing creature. Requires manual dexterity.
- **Jet** (Rage of Elements pg. 42): Can Leap up to 30 feet in any direction;
  the Leap must begin and end on a supporting solid surface.
- **Kinspeech** (Player Core pg. 212): Understands and speaks with animals of
  its species. Must be an animal, have speech, and you must be 6th level+.
- **Lab Assistant** (Core Rulebook pg. 218): Can use your Quick Alchemy action
  (you must have it; familiar in your space). Requires manual dexterity.
- **Levitator** (Rage of Elements pg. 42): Floats up to 3 feet above solid and
  liquid surfaces at Speed 25, ignoring difficult terrain and surface-contact
  effects.
- **Major Resistance** (Player Core pg. 213): Resistance from the resistance
  ability equals your level. Requires level 8+.
- **Manual Dexterity** (Player Core pg. 259): Up to two limbs act as hands for
  manipulate actions.
- **Master's Form** (Player Core pg. 213): Change shape (1 action) into a
  humanoid of your ancestry (cosmetic only). Requires manual dexterity and
  speech.
- **Partner in Crime** (Player Core pg. 213): 1 reaction per turn usable only
  to Aid Deception or Thievery; auto-success (crit if you're a master).
- **Plant** (Player Core pg. 213): Plant trait instead of animal.
- **Plant Form** (Player Core pg. 213): Change shape into a Tiny plant (as one
  with plants). Requires the plant trait.
- **Play Dead** (Tian Xia Character Guide pg. 124): Can Hide without cover
  from creatures that don't realize it's alive.
- **Poison Reservoir** (Player Core 2 pg. 170): Applies an instilled injury
  poison to an adjacent ally's weapon with one Interact. Homunculus only.
- **Resistance** (Player Core pg. 213): Choose two of acid, cold, electricity,
  fire, poison, sonic; resistance equal to half your level (min 1).
- **Scent** (Player Core pg. 259): Scent as an imprecise sense within 30 feet.
- **Second Opinion** (Player Core 2 pg. 170): 1 reaction per turn usable only
  to Aid your Recall Knowledge for a skill it has the skilled ability in;
  auto-success (crit if master).
- **Shadow Step** (Secrets of Magic pg. 229): Gains Shadow Step (teleport 30
  ft between areas of dim light/darkness). Requires level 7+.
- **Skilled** (Player Core pg. 213): Choose a skill other than Acrobatics or
  Stealth; its modifier is your level + your spellcasting attribute modifier.
  Repeatable with different skills.
- **Snoop** (Grand Bazaar pg. 34): 1 reaction per turn usable only to Aid
  Diplomacy to Gather Information; auto-success (crit if master).
- **Speech** (Player Core pg. 213): Understands and speaks a language you know.
- **Spellcasting** (Player Core pg. 213): Choose a spell you know/prepared at
  least 5 ranks below your highest slot; the familiar can Cast it once per day
  with your tradition, attack, and DC. Requires 6th-rank slots.
- **Threat Display** (Grand Bazaar pg. 34): When you Demoralize a target
  within 30 feet of the familiar, no –4 penalty for not sharing a language.
- **Toolbearer** (Player Core pg. 213): Carries a toolkit up to light Bulk;
  when adjacent, you draw/replace those tools as if wearing them. Requires
  manual dexterity.
- **Touch Telepathy** (Player Core pg. 213): Telepathic communication with you
  via touch (with speech: with any creature sharing a language).
- **Tough** (Player Core pg. 259): Max HP increases by 2 per level.
- **Tremorsense** (Grand Bazaar pg. 34): Imprecise tremorsense, 30 feet.
- **Valet** (Player Core pg. 213): Up to twice before end of your turn, it
  Interacts to place a light/negligible-Bulk worn item into your free hand.
- **Versatile Form** (Tian Xia Character Guide pg. 124): Once per day, spend
  10 minutes to change one familiar or master ability. Must be a construct;
  you must be trained in Crafting.
- **Wavesense** (Grand Bazaar pg. 34): Imprecise wavesense, 30 feet.

## Master Abilities

- **Absorb Familiar** (Player Core pg. 214): The familiar can transform into a
  mark on your flesh (1-minute activity); while transformed it can't act,
  isn't affected by area effects, and must be targeted separately (DC 20
  Perception to realize it's a creature).
- **Cantrip Connection** (Player Core pg. 214): Prepare an additional cantrip
  or add one to your repertoire. Requires cantrip casting.
- **Extra Alchemy** (Player Core 2 pg. 170): One additional advanced-alchemy
  item during daily preparations.
- **Extra Vial** (Player Core 2 pg. 170): Once per day, Interact while
  adjacent to gain a versatile vial.
- **Familiar Focus** (Player Core pg. 214): Once per day, the familiar uses 2
  concentrate actions to restore 1 Focus Point. Requires a focus pool.
- **Innate Surge** (Player Core pg. 214): Once per day, re-cast one
  ancestry-feat innate spell already cast today.
- **Kindling** (Tian Xia Character Guide pg. 124): Once per day, sacrifice the
  familiar when Casting a damaging, durationless fire Spell for a status bonus
  to its damage equal to twice the spell level.
- **Lifelink** (Player Core pg. 214): Reaction — when the familiar would drop
  to 0 HP, you take all the damage instead.
- **Recall Familiar** (Player Core pg. 214): Once per day, 3-action activity
  to teleport the familiar (within 1 mile) to your space.
- **Restorative Familiar** (Player Core pg. 214): Once per day, the familiar
  (in your space) uses 2 concentrate actions; you regain 1d8 × half your level
  HP (minimum 1d8).
- **Share Senses** (Player Core pg. 214): Every 10 minutes, 1 concentrate
  action to sense through the familiar's body for up to 1 minute.
- **Spell Battery** (Player Core pg. 214): One additional spell slot at least
  3 ranks below your highest. Requires 4th-rank slots.
- **Spell Delivery** (Player Core pg. 214): Cast a touch spell into the
  familiar; it uses its 2 actions to move to a target and deliver it.
- **Tattoo Transformation** (Grand Bazaar pg. 34): As Absorb Familiar, styled
  as a tattoo.
